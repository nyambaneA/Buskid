Executive Summary

Buzzkid Group Limited is a leading sports and wellness-oriented firm based in Nairobi, Kenya. The company is established with a mission to empower individuals through sports and has made significant strides in promoting sustainable life survival skills, with a particular focus on swimming.

The company's programs are designed to cater to individuals of all ages and abilities, offering a unique blend of physical activity and holistic wellness. Buzzkid Group Limited is registered as a limited liability company in the Republic of Kenya.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals of all ages with life-enhancing skills through sports and wellness programs, promoting a culture of health, safety, and confidence in and around water.

Our Vision

To be the leading global enterprise in sports and wellness, pioneering sustainable solutions that empower individuals and create enduring human excellence.

Our Core Values

Discipline

We believe discipline is the foundation of progress — both in and out of the pool. Every training, attendance, and performance reflects personal responsibility, time management, and respect for structure.

Commitment

We uphold consistency and dedication. Our swimmers, coaches, and partners commit to continuous growth, goal setting, and accountability in achieving both personal and team milestones.

Safety

The safety of every swimmer comes first. From beginners to advanced athletes, we maintain a culture of vigilance, certified supervision, and adherence to professional aquatic safety standards.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in coaching, technique, and character. Every session is designed to bring out the best version of each swimmer — physically, mentally, and emotionally.

Inclusion

We believe swimming is for everyone. Our programs embrace diversity and accessibility, ensuring every child, regardless of background or ability, has an opportunity to learn and grow through swimming.

Passion

We do more than teach swimming — we ignite a lifelong love for the water. Our passion drives innovation, creativity, and joy in every Buzzkid Aquatics experience.

Buzzkid Aquatics Club & Swim School

(Registered with Sports Kenya)

Buzzkid Aquatics Club is a registered swimming and aquatics training institution dedicated to building water safety, skill, and excellence in swimming. The swim school operates across multiple venues, including Syokimau, Hillpark, Heron, and Concord. It is registered with Sports Kenya as an official swimming club.

  • Learn-to-Swim Programs: Beginner-friendly lessons for all ages

  • Flexible Pack System: Sessions are offered in renewable packs of 10 for convenience and accountability
