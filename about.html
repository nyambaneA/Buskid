Who We Are

Sports for Life

Buzzkids Group Limited is a leading sports and wellness-oriented firm based in Nairobi, Kenya. Established with a mission to empower individuals through sports, Buzzkid has made significant strides in promoting sustainable life survival skills, with a particular focus on swimming.

The company's programs are designed to cater to individuals of all ages and abilities, offering a unique blend of physical activity and holistic wellness. Our approach combines technical skill development with character building, ensuring our clients not only excel in their chosen sports but also develop life skills that extend beyond the pool or field.
